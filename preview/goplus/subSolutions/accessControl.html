About Access Control

Integrated
Flexible
Accurate

An integrated end to end system that utilize RFID tags to allow compounds, residential, clubs or any other entities to control and monitor the access to their facilities in seamless manner while having perfect control, accuracy and maintaining high security level.

cutting edge technologies

RFID tag

Owners will have their tag detailed registered to allow access this will be the same tag that is used globally for the traffic management.

RFID readers

Each enter and exit point will an RFID reader that detects the Tag and forward its data the backend system.

Go+ Solution

The central software that has the access list and info which give access to registered owners and prevent unauthorized access.

Compound Management Software

An interface which allows the management to view and monitor entering and exiting activities. Also this includes a reporting and statics tool that helps display all soft of statistics needed over any period of time.

GO+ App

This application directly syncs with the backend and allows users to view their registration status it also allows owners to send invitations for their guests and visitors.

QR Invitation system

If an owner sent an invitation to a guest, the guest will receive a QR code (over their GO+ app or email) which they can use at the gates QR scanners to enter the compound.

GO+ Access Control Operational Flow:

  1. The systems rely on RFID readers at the entry point to detect RFID tag.
  2. The system will pull the data of the vehicle and check for access privileges.
  3. The facility will be notified over their management interface also developed by Go Plus of the car and owner data along with a time stamp of entry or exit.
  4. In the case of a guest the QR code will be scanned on entry and directly expires from the system, and then a secondary exit QR code will be sent to the guest to be able to exit the facility.

GO+ Access Control Benefits:

  1. Instantaneous monitoring of entrance and exit activities.
  2. Accurate vehicle and owner data.
  3. Very easy and fast to implement and register owners.
  4. Solid reporting and statics interface that allows management to create studies and analytics.
  5. GO+ application will give owners a cutting edge experience along with an e-payment solution that is hassle free.
  6. Greater flow control even within different areas in the compound as different owners can have different access levels (e.g., access to the club house ….etc.)
  7. Invitation system that makes sure only who is invited can enter and a second layer for exiting via QR codes that expires on first use. You always know who and when is come to visit whom and when they leave.
  8. An e-Wallet system that can be extended to other services and payments if desired.
  9. Overall the solution provides a complete security and access control system without the downsides of the traditional security.
