About Warehousing

Integrated
Flexible
Accurate

Go+ RFID warehouse management solution is a complete system that manages the flow of assets in and out of a warehouse and manages the location of each assets in addition to the inventory tasks. RFID provide an automatic and accurate operation that require no human intervention.

Features:

  1. WMS Functions: This solution has the standard WMS functions such as inventory definitions, max/min management etc.
  2. Easy to install: The RFID technology provide a flexible and easy solution that can be installed in any warehouse regardless of the space and structure.
  3. Eco-System: GO+ RFID WMS is easily integrated with any existing eco-system without disruptions to the existing workflow.
  4. Active Monitoring: RFID technology provide a continuous monitoring of the warehouse assets and their movement.
