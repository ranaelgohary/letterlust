About Digital Archiving

Integrated
Flexible
Accurate

Turn your business green while maintaining the safety and security of your entities’ information. Going paperless has become a thing of the future as GO+ aids in archiving and indexing documents through an all-inclusive process, which aids waste reduction while simplifying in-house processes and communication. Through OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and scanning of documents we archive and organize all the relevant information in a very short duration to for a newly established structure to you data.

Features

OCR

Go+ Digital Achieving utilizes the strongest OCR engine in order to properly capture characters and words from documents in any language.

Indexing

The solution supports document indexing and translating the indexing into proper database structure.

Compression

In order to host large amounts of data and media, Go+ Plus Digital archiving Solution utilizes strong compression engine to allow for larger data with less storage.

Document Treatment

The solution comes with a high-end image and scanning treatment engine to improve the quality of the scans in case of old or historical documentation.
