About Driving Learning Centers

Integrated
Flexible
Accurate

Great roads, vehicles and drivers are the three main elements that create the platform for safe and efficient transportation. GO+ has developed a medium into developing the driver element through its Driver Learning Centers (DLC) to aid in this progress. The DLCs have been established under numerous standards such as those developed by SGS (Société Générale de Surveillance) to assure the utmost excellence in service. Through trained and certified instructors lessons will be necessary and will deliver a set driving curriculum to teach the correct and methods to drive.

This will be complimented by developing more online sessions as well as interactive instructing through videos, online quizzes and testing Practical knowledge will be assessed as will theoretical knowledge based on Swedish Road Authorities standards, as GO+ provides testing vehicles and frameworks for all licenses as a one-stop-shop service.

Solution Elements:

  1. Learning Centers: Go+ develop and manage state-of-art learning centers utilizing high end technologies such as driving simulators, E-lecture hall and automatic services.
  2. LMS: A complete learning management system platform that manages students and their progress along with the proper and relevant assessment tools.
  3. Smart Yards: Each Center will have a smart test yard where students and testers can drive vehicles, the driving will be assessed automatically by the smart yard engine that utilizes GPS and video analytics.

Functions:

  1. Database integration: The reporting solution can connect and interface to any database from traditional SQL based databases to the modern noSQL and GraphQL databases.
  2. Report Creation: Easy to create reports for each user by simply linking data with no programming or coding needed.
  3. Live Data: The engine delivers set of dashboards that read active data.
  4. Automatic Reports: Let Go+ platform create your reports automatically at certain time periods.
  5. Forecasting: The machine learning engine will use historical data to predict and forecast future results.
  6. Discrepancies Detection: Go + machine Learning engine will detect unusual or suspicious data and alarm business managers to review it.
