    Careers

    "What to Expect" A number of new and first of its kind exciting projects to work on with a team who is at the cutting edge of implementing state-of-the-art payments solutions, working with prestigious clients alongside our widely experience partners, you'll have the opportunity to develop innovative solutions which overcome complex business challenges. On a daily basis, you will be delivering real business improvements for our partners. Ideally, you will engage in all key phases of full projects life-cycle from assessment and planning solutions to design to implementation and delivery in a relentless, not ruthless, work environment of a fast growing industry.

    Python Software Developer

    Requirements:
    ▪ Experience of 1 to 2 years
    ▪ Basic understanding of front-end technologies and platforms such as JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3
    ▪ Strong background in Python, Django, Django Rest Framework
    ▪ Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git
    ▪ Proficient understanding of OWASP security principles
    ▪ Understanding of “session management” in a distributed server environment

    Our Community Manager is our online and offline voice to the world. The role requires strong writing and communications skills, social media and public relations experience with proven Technical background.

    Requirements:
    ▪ Experience of 1 to 2 years as a Business Developer
    ▪ Holding a degree in computer Science
    ▪ Business communication skills that allows him to communicate quickly across a variety of venues and platforms
    ▪ Wide Range of communication channels and network

    Sales Executive

    Requirements:
    ▪ Experience in Fintech/Ecommerce/Technology is a must
    ▪ Excellent communicator & persuasive
    ▪ Ability to prepare presentations, proposals to corporate clients
    ▪ Excellent research and computer skills
    ▪ Self-confident and with strong presentation skills
    ▪ Great written and spoken English and Arabic
    ▪ Engineering/Science Degree is a plus

    Develops products by identifying potential products; conducting market research; generating product requirements; determining specifications, production timetables, pricing, and time-integrated plans for product introduction.

    Requirements:
    ▪ Product management, people management & understanding the customer
    ▪ Product development, requirements analysis, pricing, planning & competitive analysis
    ▪ Sales planning, inventory control, financial planning and strategy
    ▪ Experience of 1-2 Years
    ▪ A bachelor degree in Computer science

    Tech Support

    Requirements:
    ▪ Excellent problem-solving skills
    ▪ The capacity to work well within a team
    ▪ Attention to detail
    ▪ Logical thinking
    ▪ Expert knowledge in how operating systems and software works
    ▪ Interpersonal skills as you will regularly be in contact with colleagues and/or customers
    ▪ The ability to prioritise your workload
    ▪ The capacity to clearly explain a technical problem to a customer/colleague
    ▪ Outstanding listening and questioning skills

    Responsible for developing, implementing and executing strategic marketing plans for an entire organization (or lines of business and brands within an organization) in order to attract potential customers and retain existing ones.

    Requirements:
    ▪ Proven experience as marketing specialist or similar role
    ▪ Thorough understanding of marketing elements (including traditional and digital marketing such as SEO/Social media etc.) and market research methods
    ▪ Demonstrable experience in marketing data analytics and tools
    ▪ Solid computer skills, including MS Office, marketing software (Adobe Creative Suite & CRM) and applications (Web analytics, Google Adwords etc.)
    ▪ Exceptional communication and writing skills
    ▪ Commercial awareness partnered with a creative mind
