Bespoke Type-Based Designs

Letterlust is a graphic design studio with a focus on typography and lettering. Offering a wide range of design and branding services in both digital & print.

The studio specializes in creating bespoke type-based designs which convey a brand’s story with clarity and personality. Seeking to match clients with the right type of lettering, custom made to capture a brand’s identity and speak on its behalf.

Letterlust’s philosophy is to value communication above design, creating a functional, beautiful logo is equally important as a happy client whose voice was heard and well-represented in the design.

Designer Behind the Brand

After graduating with a bachelor in Applied Arts (Media Design), I went on to become a graphic designer with a passion for typography and lettering. In 2013, during my senior year, I represented the award winning website for TEDxGUC at the Awwwards Conference in Paris. After graduation I worked in London for two years at The Digital Innovation Group. Some of my experience in London includes attending the summer Typography course at Reading University.

I am currently one of three designers selected to receive a year-long mentorship with award winning type designer Nadine Chahine.

You can follow my typographic side projects on my Instagram account.
